Death Note Episodes Rankings (Top 5)

  2. Revival (Episode 24)

      3. This episode was the set up for the finale, and the set up process Light Yagami

  4. Overcast (Episode 7)

      5. In this episode Light Yagami uses his evil power, from death note, to erase
      leads that want to catch him. He got rid of the American FBI Agents, but
      now he had a new target he ahd to get rid of. This Episode is one of the best animated epsiodes out of the whole series.

  5. Wager(Episode 15)

      6. Light in this episode shows how deep his persuaion powers are. He made
